Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2025

Konjure Analytics ("we," "our," or "the app") is a sports analytics application that provides NBA and MLB player prop data, historical statistics, and parlay analysis tools for informational and entertainment purposes. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Subscriber credentials — If you create a subscriber account, we store your chosen username and a bcrypt-hashed (one-way encrypted) version of your password. Your plaintext password is never stored or transmitted in readable form.
  • Usage session data — Streamlit (our hosting platform) may log standard web server data such as IP address, browser/app user-agent, and page visit timestamps for operational and security purposes. We do not use this data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

We do not collect: real name, email address, phone number, payment information, location data, device identifiers, or any biometric data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate subscriber accounts and provide access to premium features.
  • To maintain app stability and diagnose technical issues.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating the app.

Third-Party Services

Konjure Analytics retrieves publicly available sports data from the following services. We do not share any user data with them:

  • NBA Stats API (stats.nba.com) — historical player game logs
  • MLB Stats API (statsapi.mlb.com) — historical player game logs
  • PrizePicks, Underdog Fantasy, The Odds API — current prop lines (publicly accessible endpoints)
  • Streamlit Community Cloud — app hosting platform (Streamlit Privacy Policy)

Data Storage and Security

Subscriber credentials are stored in Streamlit's encrypted secrets store. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 12) before storage — they cannot be reversed to the original password. No financial or payment data is collected or stored by this app.

Children's Privacy

Konjure Analytics is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data Retention and Deletion

Subscriber accounts can be deleted upon request. To request deletion of your account and any associated data, contact us at the email below. We will process your request within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
